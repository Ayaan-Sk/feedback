Project Overview

Laser QR Engraving

Laser-based permanent QR engraving on railway track fittings and industrial assets for indestructible identification.

Instant Scanning

Quick QR scanning to fetch real-time asset details, maintenance history, and technical specifications on the go.

Inventory Dashboard

Comprehensive management dashboard for tracking assets, scheduling maintenance, and monitoring lifecycles.

Core Features

Permanent Laser QR Marking

Deep-engraved QR codes that withstand harsh environments, grease, and friction on railway components.

Tamper-proof Identification

Real-time Inventory Tracking
